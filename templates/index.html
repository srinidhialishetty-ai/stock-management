Common challenges

Small businesses do not fail because of demand. They fail because stock moves without visibility.

Manual stock confusion

Different notebooks, chats, and files make it hard to know the real quantity on hand.

Sales and inventory disconnected

Products sell quickly, but stock counts are not reduced in time, causing overselling and poor service.

Insights arrive too late

By the time you notice slow-moving or fast-selling items, profit opportunities are already gone.
